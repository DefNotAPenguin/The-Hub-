Rocksmith+ Issues

I pre-ordered a subscription for it, I used it for a bit, and now I have issues. Major issues.

The "Buzzing"

When you first open the game, you must setup your audio devices. Since I have an interface, and the new game has built-in support, I simply setup my interface in the game.

Here's the thing. After completing your audio device setup, it forces a "noise" check, and applies noise cancellation to the instrument.

After Rocksmith applies it's noise cancellation however, there is a slightly noticable "buzz" whenever you enter a song. It's not noticable when playing, but it's slightly annoying.

Music choices

Rocksmith+ had one big thing that they advertised, and that was the sheer amount of music. To be fair, there is a LOT of music, but my problem is with the choices of music.

In Rocksmith 2014, we had a lot of fun and great songs to play, all with different difficulties. In Rocksmith+ however, the song list is a lot of songs that I don't know.
